# practice.exchange

**A stock exchange extracts. A practice exchange enriches.**

## What Is It?

A platform where you learn by doing, and your doing teaches others. You document a practice session -- growing food, tracking biomarkers, learning to weld -- and the platform turns your raw photos and narration into a standardized, depersonalized artifact that others learn from. The exchange is not transactional. You practice, you document, and that documentation flows to others who need it.

Think: YouTube Shorts, but helpful, standardized, and you do not know who made them.

## How It Works

1. **Capture.** Take photos during your practice. Add narration via voice or text.
2. **Process.** The app turns it into a short, standardized session -- captioned slideshow with background music. Faces blurred, backgrounds dimmed, format consistent. All processing happens on your phone. Nothing leaves your device until you approve it.
3. **Publish.** Your session enters the feed. It is pseudonymous -- tied to your persistent identity, but nobody knows your real name.
4. **Discover.** Browse sessions matched to your interests. Save the ones that help you. The algorithm learns from your saves and your own contributions -- never from likes, views, or engagement metrics.
5. **Connect.** After enough practice, find others on the same path. Form a circle -- a time-bound cohort with a shared social contract, start and end dates, and shared accountability. Learn together.

## What Makes It Different?

- **Sharing is the pedagogy.** Teaching what you practiced is how you internalize it. Everyone has something to share. The contribution expectation isn't a gate -- it's the learning model. Visitors get a sample. Contributors get the archive.
- **No engagement metrics.** No likes, no follows, no view counts. A "save" button feeds your own algorithm. Nobody else sees it. Ever.
- **Pseudonymous, not anonymous.** Your identity is persistent and carries reputation, but it is not tied to your legal name. Content is depersonalized. The format is the same for everyone.
- **You own your identity and data.** Cryptographic identity (DID). Social recovery through human guardians. Data exportable on demand.
- **Democratic governance.** Same fractal model from individual circles to platform constitution. Six eternity clauses that cannot be changed even unanimously.
- **Patron-funded, open financials.** Every dollar in and out is visible. Costs about $0.04/user/month at scale. No VCs. No extraction.

## What Is It Not?

- Not a social network (no feed algorithm optimizing for engagement, no followers, no performance)
- Not a course platform (no instructors, no modules, no certificates from above)
- Not a marketplace (no buying, no selling)
- A learning exchange -- a commons where practice flows between people who are all simultaneously learning and teaching

## The MVP

A PWA (no app store). Two features:
1. Session creator -- photos + narration in, standardized depersonalized artifact out, all on-device
2. The feed -- interest-matched, chronological, pseudonymous, with a contribution gate

Everything else -- circles, governance, DID, attestation -- comes after the content pipeline and feed are proven.
