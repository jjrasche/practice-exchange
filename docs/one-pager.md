# practice.exchange — One Pager

## DRAFT — needs Jim's voice, not AI's

## What is it?

A platform where you learn by doing, and your doing teaches others. You document your practice — growing food, tracking biomarkers, learning to weld — and that documentation flows into a shared exchange that others learn from. The exchange isn't transactional. A stock exchange extracts. A practice exchange enriches.

## Who is the first user?

Jim. Documenting two practices:
1. Growing food (soil blocking, indoor seeding, Zone 6a gardening) on food.practice.exchange
2. A personal neuroplasticity protocol (BDNF stack, breathwork, float tanks, psilocybin microdosing) on health.practice.exchange

## What does month one look like?

- Jim documents daily practice via omnibox input (text, photos, short video)
- The platform generates a trail — a chronological record of what was done, what was learned, what worked
- No other users yet. Class of 1. Social contract with yourself.
- Biomarker tracking begins (HRV, sleep, cognitive tests, mood)
- At end of month: one complete trail that someone else could follow to learn soil blocking or to understand the neuroplasticity protocol

## What makes it different?

- **You must contribute to consume.** Documenting your own practice is the admission price, not money.
- **No engagement metrics.** No likes, no follows, no view counts. Nobody knows what you bookmarked. Content is discoverable but people are not.
- **Connections form through learning.** No friends list. You meet people by joining the same curriculum. The social graph IS the learning graph.
- **You own your identity and data.** Cryptographic identity (DID). Social recovery through human guardians. Data exportable on demand. Platform is pipes, not a brain.
- **Democratic governance at every scale.** Same fractal governance model from individual circles to platform constitution. Six eternity clauses that cannot be changed even unanimously.
- **Patron-funded, open financials.** Every dollar in and out is visible. Costs ~$0.10/user/month at scale.

## What is it NOT?

- Not a social network (no feed, no followers, no performance)
- Not a course platform (no instructors, no modules, no certificates from above)
- Not a marketplace (no buying, no selling, no transactions)
- It's a learning exchange — a commons where practice flows between people who are all simultaneously learning and teaching
