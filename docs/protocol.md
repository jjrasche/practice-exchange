# Personal Neuroplasticity Protocol (User Zero Content)

Jim's phased self-experimentation protocol. This documentation becomes the first trail on health.practice.exchange.

## Thesis

THC removes the social calibration layer -- feels like presence but is actually hyperfocus on internal experience at the expense of relational awareness. This protocol builds prefrontal capacity instead of borrowing against it. BDNF/GDNF upregulation directly addresses ADHD structural and functional deficits.

## Neurotype Context

- ADHD + autism traits
- High working memory, pattern-building tendencies
- Tendency to intellectualize rather than feel (the "analyst trap")
- THC historically used for creative energy but removes social calibration
- Goal: build the neural infrastructure for being present with family, not just present with own experience

## Phases

### Phase 1 -- Baseline (2 weeks)
Track all biomarkers daily. No interventions. Establish measurement system.
Without baseline, everything else is anecdote.

### Phase 2 -- BDNF Stack (4 weeks)
Add simultaneously:
- Lion's mane (1-2g daily)
- Omega-3 DHA (2-4g daily)
- Cold exposure (2 min cold shower finish, daily)
- HIIT (3x/week)

Continue all biomarker tracking. Purpose: see if non-pharmacological stack moves numbers on its own before adding anything else.

### Phase 3 -- Breathwork Integration (2 weeks, overlaps end of Phase 2)
Add:
- Holotropic or extended Wim Hof sessions 2x/week
- Float tank 1x/week

Track whether breathwork days show different biomarker patterns. Sensory deprivation deepens interoception and produces theta brainwave activity overlapping with psychedelic mechanisms.

### Phase 4 -- Psilocybin Microdosing (6 weeks)
Stamets stack:
- Psilocybin 50-100mg (titrate from low -- high variance in ADHD dose sensitivity)
- Lion's mane (continue from Phase 2)
- Niacin 100mg
- Schedule: 4 days on / 3 days off (Stamets protocol, may work better for ADHD than Fadiman 1-on/2-off)

Continue all Phase 2 practices and biomarker tracking. Compare against Phase 1 baseline AND Phase 2/3 data. The stacked design isolates what microdosing adds on top of the BDNF stack.

### Phase 5 -- Macro Session (if microdosing shows signal)
- Full dose psilocybin: 2-2.5g (moderate, not heroic)
- Structured set/setting: familiar space, weighted blanket, pre-selected music, dim lighting
- Intention: "notice without narrating" (counter the analyst trap)
- Skip stimulant medication that day

Integration window (2-4 weeks post-session):
- Daily breathwork
- Float tank 2-3x/week
- Continue lion's mane + DHA
- Journaling with specific prompts (not open-ended)
- Body-based integration (movement, breathwork) over talk-based
- Build personal model of the experience AFTER feeling it (leverage autistic systemizing)

### Phase 6 -- Measurement
Compare biomarkers at 4 weeks and 8 weeks post-macro against all prior phases.
What moved? What stuck? What reverted?

## ADHD/Autism-Specific Considerations

- Stamets 4-on/3-off likely better than Fadiman 1-on/2-off (more consistent neurotrophin support)
- Start microdose lower than standard (50mg vs 100mg)
- Integration needs STRUCTURE -- calendar, alarms, accountability, specific prompts
- Hyperfocus risk during macro: may lock onto one aspect. Sitter should gently redirect.
- Rejection Sensitive Dysphoria: psychedelics can amplify shame/rejection feelings. Pre-session framing matters.
- Executive function impaired 1-3 days post-macro. Plan for reduced productivity.
- The "analyst trap": high working memory + pattern-building = intellectualizing the experience in real-time. The intention to "notice without narrating" is specifically for this.

## Key Research Findings

- Psilocybin: ~10% increase in dendritic spine density in prefrontal cortex (mouse studies), persists 1+ month
- Plasticity window: ~2 weeks for psilocybin, up to 4 weeks for ibogaine
- BDNF: ADHD associated with lower levels. Psilocybin increases expression. HIIT is strongest non-pharmacological elevator.
- GDNF: ibogaine triggers self-sustaining autoregulatory loop. Supports dopaminergic neuron survival.
- New connections are activity-dependent: what you do during the plasticity window shapes what gets rewired
- Without deliberate practice during integration, brain reverts to prior state (use-it-or-lose-it)
- No controlled clinical trials for psychedelics + ADHD yet. Mechanistic rationale is strong, evidence is survey/self-report only.
